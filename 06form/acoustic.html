Steel-String Guitar

Sometimes called a western guitar, the steel string acoustic guitar is distinct from a classical guitar in that it is strung with steel strings. These can include bronze, phosphor bronze, aluminum bronze, brass, polymer-coated, and silk and steel. These acoustic guitars are commonly played with either a pick or fingers (finger picking).
